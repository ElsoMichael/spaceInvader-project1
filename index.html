About my Game

Space Invader! I know its not a very Original Name. But its my take on a simple version of Galaga. This is for my First Project for IronHack! I picked to base my game on the old Arcade Game after completely failing at the simple mechanic of my first game that was based on the Classic Arcade Game Joyful Road. Collision with Firing is the biggest issue i faced making this game! But at the end of the day i got it to all work and i hope you enjoy it!!!!
